About Us

About the Masjid

مسجد کا تعارف

A Place of Peace & Worship

إِنَّمَا يَعْمُرُ مَسَاجِدَ اللَّهِ مَنْ آمَنَ بِاللَّهِ
"The mosques of Allah are maintained only by those who believe in Allah." — At-Tawbah 9:18

Husainiya Masjid Mehnajpur is a beloved place of worship serving the Muslim community of Mehnajpur, Lalmau, and the surrounding areas of Uttar Pradesh. Our masjid is open to all Muslims and welcomes everyone seeking guidance, prayer, and community.

We provide daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, religious education, and community programs for people of all ages. The masjid stands as a beacon of faith, unity, and spiritual growth for all.
